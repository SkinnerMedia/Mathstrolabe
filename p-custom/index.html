Custom Papers

Below is a small collection of mathematics papers which I have written for the purposes of internal assessment and teaching.

There will soon be some made publicly available, but at the moment if a lock symbol (lock) is present then papers are protected for the use of authorised institutions only. You can get your institution authorised for free by sending us a message.

I have also provided question break downs for each paper, these detail the sections of the scheme of work examined in each question (see p.73).

Paper extracts for specific questions are also available below. These are separate documents with a single question extracted from a full paper designed for ease of use.

Please also see the additional resources section if you are planning to use these papers in mock examinations.
